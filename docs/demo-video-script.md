# 90-Second Demo Video Script

## 0–10 sec: Problem
"Ops teams receive messy customer tickets across email, Slack, Jira, and CSVs. Most AI demos stop at chat, but real teams need actions, approvals, audit logs, and metrics."

## 10–25 sec: Product Overview
"This is OpsFlow AI, an AI workflow automation platform. I log into a demo workspace and upload support-ticket data."

## 25–45 sec: Ingestion + AI Suggestions
"The backend converts the CSV into normalized work items, creates embeddings, classifies each issue, and generates suggested actions such as draft reply, escalation, and internal task creation."

## 45–65 sec: Human Approval + Explainability
"Every action has an explanation: why it was suggested, what signals were used, confidence score, and payload. The user approves or rejects instead of letting AI execute blindly."

## 65–80 sec: Metrics + Audit Logs
"Approvals update metrics like time saved and approval rate. Every upload, generation, approval, and rejection is stored in the audit timeline."

## 80–90 sec: Engineering Summary
"The stack is Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL with pgvector, Redis, Docker, JWT auth, tests, and CI. This project demonstrates full-stack engineering, AI workflow design, and production-style safety controls."
